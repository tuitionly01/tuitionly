About the Program

The IGCSE Programme

The International General Certificate of Secondary Education (IGCSE) is developed by Cambridge Assessment International Education. It is the world's most popular international qualification for 14 to 16 year olds, recognised by leading universities and employers worldwide.

Our tutoring is fully aligned with the Cambridge syllabus and assessment objectives. We focus on building deep conceptual understanding that translates into strong exam performance and prepares students for AS/A Levels and beyond.
